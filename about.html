HISTORY



Kids International Kindergarten School was founded on May 19, 2010 with the objectives of addressing the Kindergarten needs of parents in Liberia.

MISSION



Our Mission at Kids International Kindergarten School is to develop the "whole Child", where the children gain confidence through the acquisition of basic skills. We establish in children love for learning, strong work ethics, creativity, respect and compassion for others. Our school follows the teaching curriculum of Ministry of Education, while incorporating modern approaches and technology in the classrooms. Our Teaching method is designed to build upon the individual skills and knowledge that will be rwquired in the Pre-school and kindergarten years. We make sure that we make most of each child's learning potentials.
